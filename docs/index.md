# Privacy Policy — 108 Again

Last Updated: April 2026

## Privacy & Data Protection (GDPR)

108 Again respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, where it is stored, and your rights regarding your personal data.

---

## Data Controller

108 Again is operated by an independent developer.

**Contact Email:**
[gian@bandieramonte.com](mailto:gian@bandieramonte.com)

---

## Data We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect:

* Email address
* Authentication credentials (securely handled through Supabase Authentication)
* Practice data, including practices, sessions, counts, and related progress history
* App settings and synchronization metadata required for proper sync functionality

If you use the app without creating an account, your data remains stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any remote server.

---

## Offline Use

108 Again is designed as an offline-first application.

You may use the app entirely without creating an account. In this case:

* your data is stored locally on your device using local database storage (SQLite)
* no cloud synchronization occurs
* no account information is collected

Creating an account is only required if you want synchronization across multiple devices or cloud backup functionality.

---

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to:

* provide synchronization between your devices
* securely store your practice progress
* authenticate your account
* support backup, restore, and account recovery
* allow account deletion and data management features

We do not:

* sell your data
* use your data for advertising
* use analytics or behavioral tracking technologies
* share your personal data with third parties except service providers required to operate the app

---

## Data Storage

Your data may be stored in two places:

### Local Storage

Data is stored locally on your device using SQLite for offline functionality.

### Cloud Storage

If you create an account, your data is securely stored using Supabase cloud infrastructure for synchronization and account recovery.

Supabase uses industry-standard security practices and encryption.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

Your data may be processed only by infrastructure providers necessary for app functionality, including:

* Supabase (authentication and database services)
* Expo (application infrastructure and build services)
* Google Play Store and Apple App Store (application distribution)

These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services.

---

## Data Retention

Your data is retained until:

* you delete your account
* you manually delete your practices or sessions
* you request deletion by contacting the developer

Some deleted information may remain temporarily in backup systems or synchronization records for technical and security purposes before permanent removal.

---

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

* access your personal data
* correct inaccurate data
* request deletion of your data
* export your data
* withdraw consent where applicable
* request restriction of processing where legally applicable

You may manage most of these actions directly inside the app, including account deletion and data export.

You may also contact the developer for assistance.

---

## Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time directly from within the app.

Deleting your account removes your cloud-synced personal data and stops future synchronization.

Local data stored on your device may also be removed depending on your actions within the app.

If your account has already been deleted from another device, the app will notify you appropriately during future sign-in attempts.

---

## Children’s Privacy

108 Again is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly target or solicit personal data from children under 13 without appropriate parental consent where required by law.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, please contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

By using the app, you understand that your data may be processed and stored through infrastructure providers that may operate in different countries, subject to applicable data protection safeguards.
